The Hog Rider card is unlocked from the Spell Valley. He is a very fast buildingtargeting, melee troop with moderately high hitpoints and damage. He appears just like his Clash of Clans counterpart a man with brown eyebrows, a beard, a mohawk, and a golden body piercing in his left ear who is riding a hog. A Hog Rider card costs 4 Elixir to deploy. Strategy His fast move speed can boost forward mini tanks like an Ice Golem in a push.
